Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes. Free cancellation is available up to 72 hours before the trip start date. Partial refunds apply after that window.

How do I modify my booking?

You can modify dates, room type, or number of travellers by contacting our support team or using the "Manage Booking" section in your dashboard.

Are flights included in the package?

It depends on the package type you select. On the Packages page, look for packages tagged as "Package with Flights" to include flights.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and net banking via all major Indian banks.

How do I get my booking confirmation?

A detailed confirmation email with vouchers and itinerary is sent to your registered email address immediately after payment.

Is my payment information safe?

Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption and are PCI-DSS compliant. We never store your card details on our servers.
